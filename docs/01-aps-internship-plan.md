# APS Internship Plan

## Role of APS

APS should be treated as:

> College -> APS -> Better Internship -> Germany -> Energy Infrastructure Company

APS gives:

- Industrial exposure
- Manufacturing understanding
- Reliability engineering experience
- Access to QA/process engineers
- Project material for CV
- Possible data for research
- Credibility for future internships

The frame is not "I am becoming a solar manufacturing guy."

The frame is:

> I am using PV manufacturing to learn industrial reliability, quality analytics, imaging, statistics, and engineering discipline.

## Reliability Concepts

Study:

- Microcracks
- Hotspots
- IV curves
- LID
- LeTID
- PID
- Ribbon fatigue
- Delamination
- EVA discoloration
- Moisture ingress
- Cell mismatch
- Busbar interruption

Important distinction:

- Microcracks are mostly mechanical. They can cause current mismatch, hotspots, and power loss.
- PID is electrical degradation related to high-voltage stress, leakage current, and ion migration.
